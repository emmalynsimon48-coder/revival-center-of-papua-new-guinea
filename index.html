Our Journey

The Revival Center of Papua New Guinea began in 1982 in Lumi, starting with 28 baptisms and quickly spreading across villages in the highlands.

Under the guidance of Pastor Godfrey Wippon and other pioneering souls, the message of revival transformed entire communities.

Our History

Revival Centres Of Papua New Guinea (RCPNG) is a pentecostal church. We are affiliated with Revival Fellowships worldwide. We preach Salvation Message as found in the book Acts chapter 2 verse 38. We emphasise on the necessity for one to be born-again of water and the Holy Spirit. The Revival Center of Papua New Guinea began in 1982 in Lumi, starting with 28 baptisms and quickly spreading across villages. We express God's love through practical service to our neighbors and nation.
Our Purpose is to bring hope and salvation to the people of Papua New Guinea.

Our Church

We have churches across Papua New Guinea, from the highlands to the coastal regions, each serving as a beacon of hope and transformation in their communities. At our church we have different ministries that cater to the needs of our members and the community. We have a vibrant youth ministry that engages young people in worship, discipleship, and outreach activities. We also have a women's ministry that empowers and supports women in their faith journey and life challenges. And we have a men ministry that provides spiritual guidance and fellowship for men.
